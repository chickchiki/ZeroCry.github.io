"If You want To Lift Yourself Up, Lift Up Someone Else"

The application "ZeroCRY" propagates the ideology that “one can't help everyone but everyone can help someone “ and “help isn’t always compulsive to be monetary. “The application promotes collaborations among NGOs, equal distribution of wealth and enlightens communities.

About ZeroCRY

Aiming at the sustainable development goal of “No Poverty” and “Zero Hunger”, android development has been employed integrated with other technicalities to connect a huge audience base and promote coordinated progress. The fully featured android application, “ZeroCRY” , precisely connects the philanthropic and affluent donators directly to the deprived ones ,eradicating corruption. This application is a multiscreen application with a user friendly interface and is easy to use. To authenticate the users' need for the aids, the non government organisations have been involved in it. The application grabs everyone’s attention with its special feature that the donator can easily access the categories of needs of the impoverished ones like clothes,meals,medication,money and education.ZeroCRY promotes transparency in social work by providing the list of the registered NGOs whom any commoner can approach for any purport. For any commoner , ZeroCRY is the perfect technical solution by means of which they can contact any NGO either for performing any voluntary charity ,seeking any personal or communal help or for being a member in the organization.Any person who desires to fund any charity show can also be an user of this application.If a person is in any dire situation,can register into this application and use it for seeking help. Besides all these exciting facilities, ZeroCRY attempts to aware the citizens about the schemes sponsored by the Government. The users can directly enroll and avail these schemes from the application.Any user loves to be in personal contact with the NGO or the person whom he/she wishes to help or seek help from.This app thus facilitates a chat section for the users of the application. This is ZeroCRY,the one stop application for the donators,the needy and the NGOs.

Backdrop

The progressing decades have witnessed vital transmogrification in the civilizations. Malnutrition, illiteracy , destitution and inequalities subdue the scientific and technological advancements .Global threats like COVID-19 attempt to reverse progress in fighting global poverty. “Poverty is the parent of revolution and crime.” ~Aristotle The catastrophic growth of poverty and chronic hunger highlights that its reason is the paucity of access to the resources. Inadequate retrievement of clean freshwater and dearth of nutritious meals degrade physical and mental health, originate water-borne illnesses, increase medicinal costs , thus making the impoverished families more vulnerable. Lack of availability of jobs and exploitation of the traditional livelihoods like farming, fishing and many more ( due to climate changes, conflicts and overpopulation ) cause monetary drawbacks. Sometimes poor infrastructure and absence of Internet isolate the communities and make them incapable to fetch the fundamental provisions. Travelling to distant locations for trading, schooling and earning hike travel expenses and cause time and energy drain. Researches interpret education and literacy are the sole revolutionising tools to extirpate social and economic inequalities. Few to no schools in some regions, incompetence to pay education fees and lack of awareness prohibit education from marginalising gender ,caste and race discriminations . History unveils how wars and internal discords have demolished financial stability, communal infrastructures and caused major deaths. Collective reasons induce absolute and relative poverty, causing economic downfall, child labour and women’s sexual exploitation. Ineffective governments fail to prevent these miserable families from backsliding into further meagre conditions. ZeroCRY attempts to personally approach each and every poverty-stricken nooks of the society and lend a helping hand to them to uprise and progress.ZeroCRY targets equal financial thrift amongst the citizens.Its time to eradicate poverty and promote equality.This led us to create ZeroCRY.
